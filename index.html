SDPVO covariance & weight analysis

Per-keyframe uncertainty diagnostics for stereo DPVO on the M3ED dataset. Each run combines (a) BA-Hessian marginal covariance Σ_pos, (b) factor-head patch weights W, and (c) Sim(3)-aligned GT to produce correlations of Σ/W features against relative pose error.

TL;DR

  • Σ shape is reliable; Σ magnitude is not. Mean NEES is several orders of magnitude above its 6-DOF target, yet Sim(3) alignment scale stays within a few percent of 1 — so Σ is intrinsically under-estimated, not distorted by post-hoc scaling.
  • Scale-invariant features lead: fusion_max, fusion_combined_sum, cond_pos, max_eig_pos all reach median |Spearman r| ≈ 0.31–0.34 vs RPE-trans.
  • Raw trace / confidence are weak (median |r| ≈ 0.22). Do not use them for per-keyframe gating.
  • Per-axis σd correlates with |ed| for translation and y-rotation, enabling per-component EKF gating rules even when the trace summary is noisy.
  • Config difference is small (<0.05 in median |r| between default and superfast across every top metric).
